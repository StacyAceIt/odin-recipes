Kefir

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1 tablespoon of kefir grains
    •

Recipe Instructions

  1. Pour the milk into a clean glass jar.
  2. Add the kefir grains to the milk.
  3. Cover the jar with a cloth or coffee filter and secure it with a rubber band.
  4. Let the mixture sit at room temperature for 24-48 hours, stirring occasionally.
  5. Once the milk has thickened and tastes tangy, strain the kefir into another clean jar, using a non-metal strainer to catch the kefir grains.
  6. Store the strained kefir in the refrigerator and enjoy within a week.
  7. Use the kefir grains to start a new batch by repeating the process.
